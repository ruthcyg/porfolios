About Me

I am a Nurse turned Full-Stack Web Developer with a passion for technology and writing. My development expertise spans technologies such as Node.js, JavaScript, React, React Native, TypeScript, CSS, HTML, MongoDB, Linux Administration, Git, and SQL. Additionally, I am an accomplished author of two technical books, "Lambda Function: Python's Playbook" and "Per Minute: The Power of One Minute at a Time," which showcase my ability to demystify complex concepts for a wide audience. I have also published over 57 articles on Medium covering a range of topics from Python to React, further establishing my skills in technical communication and thought leadership.
